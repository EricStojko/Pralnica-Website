Solution for your laundry

We know that doing laundry on the road can be a challenge. Our industrial 18 kg machines are perfect for washing bedding, towels, and sleeping bags from your motorhome.

The entire wash and dry cycle takes only 45-60 minutes, meaning you'll be back on the road in no time.

Parking Guide for Koper

Accessing self-service laundries in city centers can be difficult with larger vehicles. We have a proven strategy for you:

"Partner Drop-off" Strategy

One person hops out with the laundry at the nearest bus stop or delivery zone (only 50m away), while the driver parks at one of the larger parking areas:

  • Market Parking (Tržnica): Large parking lot nearby.
  • PZA Koper (Motorhome Parking): Dedicated spot for you, just a short walk away.
