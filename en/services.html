What We Don't Wash

For hygiene and safety reasons, we do not accept: pet clothing and blankets (full of hair), work clothing, footwear (trainers/shoes), kitchen and floor cleaning rags, and sports equipment. If you're unsure whether your item is suitable, please ask us.
