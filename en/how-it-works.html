How to Wash & Dry

💶 TOKEN MACHINE

1€ = 1 Token

The machine accepts banknotes and coins of 0.50€, 1€, 2€, 5€, 10€, and 20€.

1. WASHING

1

Preparation

Select an available machine (9kg or 18kg) and load laundry. Close door firmly.

2

Exchange & Pay

Exchange cash for tokens at the token machine (1€ = 1 Token).
Insert tokens:
9kg = 5 tokens
18kg = 10 tokens

3

Start

Select temperature and press the green START button. The machines automatically dispense professional detergent and softener for normally soiled laundry.

Do you have allergies or very dirty laundry and want to use your own detergent? Let us know and we will disable the automatic dispenser!

2. DRYING

1

Preparation

Load laundry into dryer (14kg).

2

Tokens

Insert tokens (1 token = 8 min drying). We recommend at least 4 tokens for optimal results.

3

Start

Select temperature and press START. You can open the door during cycle to check dryness.

Time Estimates

Washing: 40-50 minutes

Drying: ~32 minutes (regular) to 50 minutes (duvets)

Washed and dried in approx. 1.5 hours
